"All my life, I've Had one dream: to achieve my many goals." - Homer Simpson


Hi, my name is Ryan Lambert and I am an Interactive Multimedia Developer from Ottawa, Canada. I will graduate with a Bachelor of Information Technology in Interactive Multimedia and Design (a program between Carleton University and Algonquin College) in December 2017 and I am ready to achieve the many goals that I have set for myself. The IMD program has set me up to be able to pursue any number of careers, ranging from web development to game design to IOT technologies. I want to do it all.

I originally planned on moving towards game development, however I recently became enthralled with the possibilities of the Internet of Things. I would love to combine the 2 into a new way of gamifying the everyday world. I love experimenting with these concepts and plan to upload my projects here as I explore the limitless possibilities that this route promises.

Recently, I have also decided to expand my knowledge of web technologies, and I plan to use this site as a way to display the evolution of my skills as a web developer. The current iteration was developed using HTML, SASS, jQuery, and a bit of AJAX. It took me about 3 weeks off-and-on as I refreshed my knowledge of these tools. Hopefully the next iteration will be much more interactive.

In the meantime, please take the time to explore my past projects, and send me a message if you have any questions or if you think we would work well together!
