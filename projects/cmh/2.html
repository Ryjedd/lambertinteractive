Multitouch Pedestal Sample

Goal

Experiment with the Intuiface software by developing a unique and fun way of presenting information about artifacts within an exhibit on a multitouch display.

Skills/Tools Used

Intuiface, Photoshop

About the Project

(July 2016)

This sample was based on a grander idea I had, involving placing replica artifacts on a physical pedestal to activate unique experiences within an exhibit. I still hope to develop a prototype of that system someday, however this was my attempt to create a digital version. I wanted to create an interface where different artifacts could be placed on a central pedestal, which would present content unique to that artifact. This was another introductory excercise to the Intuiface software.

What I Gained from this Project

This project allowed me to be creative by continuing to experiment with unique interactions in a multitouch environment. I was given no specific instructions, except to experiment with the Intuiface software and evaluate its potential use in developing content for exhibits.
